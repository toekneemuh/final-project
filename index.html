w3-row and w3-container

While the w3-row class defines a padding-less container, when you also have w3-container, it adds 16-pixel padding around the entire row, but not between the contained elements:

<section class="w3-container w3-row">
<article class="w3-col s12 m4 l4">

w3-row-padding only

The w3-row-padding class adds an 8px left and right padding to each column. This gives the row of images both margins and gutters:

<section class="w3-row-padding">
<article class="w3-col s12 m4 l4">
